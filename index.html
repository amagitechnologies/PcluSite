For All Filipinos

We are a non-partisan and non-profit association of legal professionals and community members concerned about preserving our rights. We endeavor to ensure that all Filipinos are treated equally under the law.

Defending Our Rights

Whether we're creating new juris prudence to better distinguish the rights of minority groups; supporting new privacy protections in the modern, digital age; seeking justice for wrongly persecuted people; or preserving the right to a free press; the PCLU takes up the toughest civil liberties cases and issues to defend all people from government abuse and overreach.

Bill of Rights

Our rights need constant defending from an encroaching, and oftentimes corrupt, government. Rights violations today come in the form of vigilante murders, unjust searches and seizures, or oppression and intimidation of specific groups of people to name just a few.


Our primary focus is on Human Rights, Prisoners' Rights, Women's Rights, Free Speech, and Digital Privacy.

Government Oversight

It's generally accepted that the Filipino government is filled with bad actors. It is the obligation of the people to keep them in check.


Our primary focus is on Juvenile Justice, Rights of the Accused, and Government Transparency.

Legal Education

The PCLU is currently developing programs to increase the skill set of our legal professional members and the knowledge of our supporters. Education is a crucial ingredient to building an informed and capable society, so we see this as a crucial part to our overall mission. If you would like to offer a course or have a specific need that you would like us to focus on, please contact us via email.

Affiliate Chapters

We currently operate primarily in Manila. If you want to host your own Affiliate Chapter in your province, send us an email, and we'll begin the process of helping you submit the below details to start the process:

  • Contact details of proposed Board members
  • Official chapter office address

Support the PCLU

The PCLU is only as strong as the team supporting us. If you're an attorney, we will provide you a strong network and resources to right wrongs throughout the country. As a community supporter, we'll work to put your donations and time to use in creating a safer and better world for everyone.
